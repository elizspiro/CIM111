PLACES TO SEE & THINGS TO DO

Boston is home to many historical landmarks and scenic views that are picturesque in any season. Your options for exploring the city are endless! Below are my top places to visit and things to do during your trip.



Public Gardens and Boston Commons

Take a stroll through the Public Gardens and Boston Commons- the oldest public park in the United States! This beatifully landscaped park is like the Central Park of Boston. In warmer months you can take a ride on the iconic swan boats, and in the winter you can ice skate on Frog Pond.

Charles River Esplinade

The Esplinade is another popular destination, with views of the Charles River. It is perfect for a scenic jog or bike ride, and you can even rent kayaks and paddleboards if you want to get out on the water. The hatshell

Newbury Street

Newbury street is made up of eight blocks that stretch through downtown Boston and the Back Bay. It is lined with many stores, boutiques, salons, restaurants, cafes, and bars. You will find people dining outdoors in the warmer months, and shopping at the wide range of retailers no matter the season.
