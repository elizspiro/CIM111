PLACES TO SEE & THINGS TO DO

This is an intro section for this page, will be the first thing viewers probably read

Public Gardens and Boston Commons

Take a stroll through the Public Gardens and Boston Commons- the oldest public park in the United States! This beatifully landscaped park is like the Central Park of Boston. In warmer months you can take a ride on the iconic swan boats, and in the winter you can ice skate on Frog Pond.

Charles River Esplinade

The Esplinade is another popular destination, with views of the Charles River. It is perfect for a scenic jog or bike ride, and you can even rent kayaks and paddleboards if you want to get out on the water.

North End

The North End is Boston's Little Italy, featuring sites like the Old North Church and the Paul Revere house. Get authentic Italian food and definitely don't skip dessert at Mike's Pastry, famous for their delicious cannolis.
