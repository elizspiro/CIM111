Learn how to bake a classic birthday cake that takes under an hour! Today we will be show you the step by step process to create a delicious Funfetti vanilla sponge cake with rich chocolate frosting. Decorate it to fit any special occassion and be the hit of the party! Simple and tasty!

click to show/hide INGREDIENTS step

The first step is gathering all the ingredients. Here is a lsit of what you will need:

  • Funfetti vanilla cake mix
  • Three large eggs
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup Water
  • Chocolate frosting

    • Set your oven to 350ºF. Coat bottom of pan with no stick cooking spray or shortening and a flour dusting.

click to show/hide MIXING step

After gathering everything you will need to bake the cake, combine the ingredients into a large bowl. Beat cake mix, water, oil, and eggs in bowl with mixer on medium speed for 2 minutes- or hand whisk if you don't have.

click to show/hide PREPARING PAN step

Here we begin step 3: Pour the batter into your pre-greased pan. Depending on what size you may need more than one pan.

click to show/hide BAKING step

Bake at 350ºF. We used a square 8x8-inch pan and baked for 40-45 minutes, but all ovens vary. When toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean, cake is done. Cool 10 to 15 minutes before removing from the pan.

click to show/hide FROSTING step

Cool cake completely before frosting. Use any flavor frosting you desire. We decided to use milk chocolate frosting and spread it over the entire cake.

click to show/hide the FINAL PRODUCT

This is the final product! We decorated the cake with multicolored sprinkles and candles to celebrate a 21st birthday.
